About PetConnect

Promoting Responsible Pet Adoption & Animal Welfare

What is PetConnect?

PetConnect is an online pet care and adoption system designed to promote responsible pet ownership, ethical adoption practices, and animal welfare awareness. The platform serves as a digital bridge connecting individuals, animal shelters, and potential adopters in a centralized and accessible environment.

Our Key Features

  • Free Pet Listing: Authorized individuals and registered animal shelters can list pets for adoption free of charge. Users with proper access can add, update, and manage pet records directly within the system, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for prospective adopters.
  • Pet Management: This feature empowers shelters and individuals to actively participate in the adoption process while maintaining control over their listed pets.
  • Educational Resources: PetConnect provides reliable information related to proper pet care, health awareness, and responsible ownership. By integrating adoption services with educational resources, the system supports informed decision-making among users and encourages long-term commitment to animal care.
  • Community Engagement: Developed as an academic project under Cavite State University ‚Äì Bacoor City Campus, PetConnect demonstrates the practical application of web technologies in addressing real-world social concerns.

Our Mission

The system highlights the importance of technology-driven solutions in supporting animal welfare, community engagement, and sustainable adoption initiatives. We believe that every pet deserves a loving home, and every potential adopter deserves the tools and information to make the right choice.
